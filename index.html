Patrick's Coding Adventure

Welcome to the beginning!

As with all good Hero’s Journey’s we begin with the tranquil setting of the home page. From here you will answer the call to adventure and face the perilous dangers of my blog entries, all accessible by traversing through the portal that is the side bar.

But first we must paint the scene and tell you a little of our protagonist.

My name is Patrick and I live in Tawa, near Wellington.

I am attending De Academy as I coding has always been a passion of mine and something I have always enjoyed doing in my spare time. have dabbled in coding from an early age, initially in GameMaker and later in Unity (with a fair amount of VBA to make overly complicated excel spreadsheets!). I trained to be a doctor because I like helping people and I currently work in mental health. The job can be rewarding but stressful! I want to now try and find something that will sustain me in the long term and try to make a career out of something I choose to do as a hobby!
